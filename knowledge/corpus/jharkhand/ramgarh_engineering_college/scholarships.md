# Scholarships — Ramgarh Engineering College

> **Source:** Synthetic Benchmark Corpus v1.0 | **Confidence:** Generated | **Date:** 2026-07-12

## Available Scholarships

- Jharkhand Government EWS Scheme
- Jharkhand Minority Scholarship Scheme
- E-Kalyan Jharkhand Post-Matric Scholarship
- Post-Matric Scholarship for SC/ST/OBC Students (e-Kalyan)
- Birsa Munda Merit Scholarship
- Merit-cum-Means Scholarship (Jharkhand State)

## Application
Most scholarships are applied for through the **e-Kalyan Portal (ekalyan.cgg.gov.in)** for Jharkhand state schemes, or through the National Scholarship Portal. Students should apply within 30 days of the commencement of the first semester.

## Additional Support
The institute has a Student Welfare Fund to provide emergency financial assistance to students in genuine need. Applications can be made to the Dean of Student Welfare at any time.
