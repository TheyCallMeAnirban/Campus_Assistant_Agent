## Payment
All fees are payable online through the institute's student portal. A late payment penalty of ₹500/week applies beyond the due date.

## Fee Waivers
SC/ST students are eligible for a full tuition fee waiver subject to Jharkhand state government norms. EWS students may apply for fee concession through the National Scholarship Portal or Jharkhand e-Kalyan portal.
